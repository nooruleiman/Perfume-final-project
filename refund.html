Refund Policy

At Olfactory Oasis, we strive to ensure that you are completely satisfied with your purchase. If you are not entirely happy with your order, we're here to help. Please read our refund policy carefully:

Eligibility for Refunds

  • Timeframe: Refund requests must be submitted within 14 days of receiving your order.
  • Condition of Items:
    • The perfume bottle must be unused, unopened, and in its original packaging.
    • Items that are damaged due to misuse or not in their original condition will not be eligible for a refund.
  • Proof of Purchase: A valid receipt or order confirmation is required to process your refund.

Non-Refundable Items

We cannot process refunds for the following:

  • Opened or used perfume bottles.
  • Sale or clearance items (unless defective).
  • Customized or personalized items.

How to Request a Refund

  1. Contact us at olfacotryoasis@gmail.com with:
    • Your order number.
    • Details of the issue.
    • Photos of the item, if applicable.
  2. Our team will review your request and respond within 3 business days.

Return Process

  1. Approval: Once your refund is approved, we will provide instructions for returning the product.
  2. Shipping Costs:
    • Customers are responsible for return shipping costs unless the item was defective or incorrect.
    • We recommend using a trackable shipping method to ensure safe delivery.

Processing Refunds

Once your return is received and inspected, we will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund. If approved, your refund will be processed, and the amount will be credited to your original payment method within 7-10 business days.

Exchanges

We do not offer direct exchanges. If you wish to exchange an item, please return it for a refund and place a new order.
